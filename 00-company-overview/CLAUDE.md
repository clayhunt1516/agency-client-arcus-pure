## Conventions

- When a company-overview fact is unknown, mark it `[discovery]` and log it as a discovery question (Notion → Discovery Questions) — do not guess.
- When a claim is contradicted by primary-source evidence, mark it `[verify]` and resolve by replacing the claim with the correct content — not by supplementing it.
- **DBA capture.** When recording a vendor, subcontractor, or other counterparty that operates under a "doing business as" (DBA) name, capture both names — the operating (trading) name and the registered legal entity. In roster tables this is the Operating Name / Legal Entity column pair; in prose, name both explicitly. Recording both lets the brain match a counterparty whether a source document uses its trading name or its legal name.
